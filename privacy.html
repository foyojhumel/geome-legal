Privacy Policy for geoME

Last updated: June 17, 2026

geoME is a mobile review application that provides access to review questions, solutions, and performance analytics to help users identify their weak areas. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the geoME mobile application.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account information, such as email address and user profile details.
  • Authentication information handled through Supabase.
  • Quiz activity, review progress, scores, answers, and performance analytics.
  • Subscription and purchase status handled through RevenueCat and Google Play Billing.
  • Basic device, app, and diagnostic information needed to maintain and improve the app.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Provide review questions, solutions, and analytics.
  • Track learning progress and identify weak topics.
  • Verify premium access and subscription status.
  • Improve app reliability, security, and user experience.

Third-Party Services

geoME may use third-party services to operate core app features:

  • Supabase for authentication, database, and user data storage.
  • RevenueCat for subscription entitlement management.
  • Google Play Billing for Android subscription and in-app purchase processing.

Payments

Payments for subscriptions or in-app purchases are processed by Google Play Billing and managed through RevenueCat. We do not directly collect or store your credit card or payment account details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data with service providers only as needed to provide authentication, database, analytics, subscription, payment verification, security, and app functionality.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect user data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the app, maintain user accounts, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our terms.

User Rights and Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and operational requirements.

Children's Privacy

geoME is intended for users preparing for examinations and is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
